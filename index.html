2021 Movie Quiz

This website, developed by myself (Matthew Gibson)is a basic project which will use HTML, CSS and JavaScript to implement a basic website which incorporates an interactive quiz based on the topic of 2021 movies.

The focus of the quiz will be on movies that were released in 2021. The quiz will focus on popular movies such as Dune, Spider-Man No Way Home, Free Guy, Raya and the Last Dragon, and Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings, and other movies such as these so that most people who keep up to date with modern movies could confidently participate in this quiz.

For each question there will be multiple choices presented to the you (the user) to which you have to select which one you believe is correct. An example question would be - Who was the actor who played Paul Atreides (the main character) in Denis Villeneuve’s re-imagination of Frank Herberts 1965 classic novel called ’Dune’? - Timothee Chalamet.
